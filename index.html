About Me

My name is Sayana Adhikari. I am a senior at the University of Kentucky, majoring in Information, Communication, and Technology with a minor in Business. I grew up in Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, before moving to the United States.

I migrated to the United States at the age of 15. At first, it was challenging to adjust to a new culture, language, and education system. However, over time, I learned how to adapt and grow in this new environment.

Being an international student has helped me become more independent, responsible, and confident. I have learned how to manage my time, balance school and personal life, and stay focused on my goals.

My Academic Journey

At the University of Kentucky, I have worked hard to improve my academic skills. My major in Information, Communication, and Technology allows me to learn about technology, communication, and digital systems.

I enjoy learning how websites, software, and digital tools are created and used. These skills are important in today’s technology-driven world.

Achievements

One of my proudest achievements is being a Dean’s List recipient. This shows my dedication to my studies and my commitment to doing my best.

I am grateful for my professors and classmates who have supported me throughout my academic journey. Their encouragement motivates me to continue improving every day.
