Interest in Painting

Recently, I have become interested in learning watercolor painting. I enjoy expressing my creativity through art and exploring new skills.

Painting helps me stay calm and focused. In the future, I hope to improve my artistic abilities.

Travel Experiences

Traveling is another hobby that I truly enjoy. Visiting new places allows me to learn about different cultures and meet new people. In the past few years, I have travelled to more than 10 states. Now, my next goal is to travel to new countries.

Coming from a landlocked country, I love beach. One of my favorite trips was to Myrtle Beach. The peaceful environment and beautiful ocean view helped me relax and enjoy nature.

Sports Achievements

Sports play an important role in my life. I used to play badminton and basketball in middle school and first year of high school, but not anymore. I wish to pick up on badminton again. Hopefully, I can manage that this year.

I have won a silver medal in badminton and a gold medal in basketball. These achievements are very precious to me. They motivate me to do better.
